Cake War

Overview

Cake War is a survival Bedwars with insane Vanilla items, where you have to mine to get resources. Protect your cake as much as possible, and go in search of the ennemies'. This datapack is heavy, so make sure you host it on a powerful server. You can download the adaptable datapack to play on your own map, or use a prepared map which already contains the datapack and structures.
